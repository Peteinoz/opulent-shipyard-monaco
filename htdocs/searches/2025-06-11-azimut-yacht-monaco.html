AVA Summary

Several Azimut yachts have a notable presence in Monaco, especially highlighted during events like the Monaco Yacht Show. In 2024, Azimut showcased three prominent models at the Monaco Yacht Show: the Magellano 30M, Grande 36M, and Grande Trideck. These yachts represent the brand's commitment to luxury, design innovation, and state-of-the-art technology. The Grande Trideck, at 38 meters, stands out as the flagship model, designed for modern owners seeking a blend of conviviality and a strong connection to the surrounding sea. The Magellano 30M features a striking design with a panoramic 270° sea view on the main deck. Additionally, the Azimut 72, a 22.61-meter yacht, is available for sale through Monaco-based brokers and features advanced technology, high performance, and luxurious comfort.
