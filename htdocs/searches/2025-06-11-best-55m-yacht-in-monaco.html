AVA Summary

The best 55m yacht currently in Monaco is the freshly delivered 2025 Heesen yacht 'Serena'. Measuring exactly 55 meters, Serena offers a blend of modern Dutch engineering and luxury. Notable features include elegant exterior lines by Omega Architects, interiors by LUCA DINI Design & Architecture, expansive windows for panoramic views, a sun deck with spa and bar, and a beach club with its own sauna. The yacht accommodates 10 guests in 5 cabins and is operated by a crew of 13. Launched in August 2024 and delivered in March 2025, Serena is built to the highest Lloyds Register technical standards. Its fast displacement steel hull and aluminium superstructure provide both comfort and performance, with a cruising speed of 13 knots and a top speed of 15.5 knots. Serena stands out for her refined finish, unique marble in each stateroom, and exceptional onboard amenities, making her a top choice among 55m yachts cruising Monaco in 2025.
