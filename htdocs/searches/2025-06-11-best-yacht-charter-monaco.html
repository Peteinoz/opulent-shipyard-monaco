AVA Summary

Monaco is among the premier destinations for luxury yacht charters, offering a wide variety of motor and sailing yachts suitable for every budget and preference. Charter guests can expect personalized itineraries, five-star service, and access to the top destinations along the French Riviera, from Villefranche-sur-Mer and St. Tropez to Nice and Cannes. The experience typically includes a private chef, tailored amenities, and access to exclusive events such as the Monaco Grand Prix. Charter prices for Monaco start from as low as $350 per day for smaller boats and can exceed $500,000 per week for high-end superyachts, excluding expenses. Top providers catering to Monaco charters include YachtCharterFleet, IYC, Fraser Yachts, Boatcrowd, and GetMyBoat. Most brokers offer options for both short trips and extended charters, accommodating groups ranging from families to high-net-worth individuals seeking bespoke experiences.
