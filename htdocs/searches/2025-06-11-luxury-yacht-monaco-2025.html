In 2025, Monaco will once again be a focal point for luxury yachting, especially during the iconic Monaco Grand Prix from May 22 to 25. Several world-class luxury yachts are confirmed to be present, including standout vessels such as Lionheart (Benetti), celebrated for her immense scale, helipad, full wellness center, and opulent interiors, and O’Ptasia, which recently underwent a major refit and offers expansive entertainment amenities, a beach club, hammam, and cinema-equipped suites. Both accommodate large parties with high crew-to-guest ratios, offering the utmost in comfort and exclusivity. Premier charter brokers and ticket agencies are offering bespoke packages to experience race week aboard these or similar vessels in Monaco’s famed port.
