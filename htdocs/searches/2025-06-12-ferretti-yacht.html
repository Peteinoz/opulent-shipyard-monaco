AVA Summary

Ferretti Yachts offers a range of luxury flybridge yachts, celebrated for their blend of comfort, safety, innovative design, and advanced technology. Key models in their lineup include the Ferretti Yachts 1000, 780, and 720. - The Ferretti Yachts 1000 is a 100-foot flagship yacht, providing exceptional versatility, spacious interiors with two design moods, complete privacy for owners and guests, and an innovative aft beach area with a large flybridge. - The Ferretti Yachts 780, with a length of about 78 feet 9 inches, also stands out for its luxury and advanced engineering, accommodating up to 20 people, and offering high speeds with its MAN V12 engines. - The Ferretti Yachts 720, at 73 feet 2 inches, features unique interior solutions, a hardtop or sun deck version, and superior cruising comfort.
