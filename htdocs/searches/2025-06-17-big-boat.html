Somnio: World's Largest Superyacht

Somnio is expected to become the world's largest superyacht upon its eventual completion, measuring an astounding 222m (728ft) in length. Somnio will include 30 luxury private residences, all of which can only be acquired through invitation only, and will feature an extensive array of entertainment and leisure facilities.
